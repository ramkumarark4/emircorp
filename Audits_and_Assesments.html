BUSINESS HISTORY AND OVERVIEW

PEERSENG, founded in 2016 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, specializes in engineering consultancy and risk management for the oil and gas industry. Over 8 years, we have earned a reputation for delivering innovative solutions across India, the Middle East, Europe, and the APAC region, prioritizing safety, efficiency, and operational excellence.

In July 2024, we expanded to Sharjah, UAE, with EmirCorp LLC, extending our expertise to the Middle Eastern market.

Together, PEERSENG and EmirCorp LLC bring localized expertise and global experience, offering tailored solutions to meet the complex needs of the energy sector worldwide
