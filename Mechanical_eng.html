Our Services Include:

  • Mechanical and piping systems and material diagram and equipment selection, sizing, and evaluation

    • Mechanical and piping systems involve the design and arrangement of components that transport fluids or gases in industrial settings. Material diagrams map out the flow paths and equipment, ensuring clarity in system design. Equipment selection, sizing, and evaluation focus on choosing the right machinery and components, determining their appropriate dimensions, and assessing their performance to meet operational and safety requirements efficiently.

  • Equipment drawings, P&IDs,general arrangement drawings, piping, and equipment module drawings

    • These involve creating detailed technical diagrams, including piping and instrumentation diagrams (P&IDs), layout plans, and module-specific drawings for equipment and piping. These drawings ensure accurate visualization, system integration, and compliance with project requirements.

  • Equipment work packages (EWPs)

    • EWPs are comprehensive documents that outline the scope, tasks, resources, and timelines required for equipment installation and commissioning. They provide a structured approach to ensure seamless execution, safety compliance, and operational efficiency.

  • Equipment datasheets and specifications

    • Datasheets and specifications provide detailed technical information about equipment, including performance parameters, materials, and dimensions. These documents guide procurement, installation, and operation while ensuring compliance with project requirements.

  • Material requisitions (MRQs), technical bid evaluations (TBEs), and purchase orders (POs) for equipment and services

    • This process involves preparing material requisitions, evaluating vendor bids for technical suitability, and issuing purchase orders. It ensures that the selected equipment and services meet project specifications, budgets, and timelines.

  • Vendor drawing reviews following applicable codes and standards

    • Vendor drawing reviews ensure that supplier-provided designs and documentation meet project specifications, industry standards, and applicable codes. This step ensures quality control and alignment with project objectives.

  • Research scale pilot plants for sustainable energy and emission recovery projects

    • These projects involve designing and developing small-scale pilot plants to test and validate sustainable energy and emission recovery technologies. The goal is to optimize processes before full-scale implementation.

  • Review and revise customer’s specification for code compliance, optimizations and to match with the developed technologies

    • This involves analyzing customer-provided specifications to ensure compliance with relevant codes and standards, recommending optimizations, and aligning them with new or developed technologies to improve project outcomes.

  • Wellsite equipment and piping modules

    • This includes designing modularized equipment and piping systems for wellsite operations, ensuring easy installation, transportability, and efficient operation in oil and gas exploration and production activities.

  • Enhanced oil recovery systems

    • Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) systems involve using advanced techniques like gas injection, chemical flooding, or thermal recovery to maximize oil extraction from reservoirs, improving efficiency and extending field life.

  • Modularization of plant equipment and piping

    • Modularization involves designing plant systems as prefabricated modules to simplify installation, reduce construction time, and improve project efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

  • Tank farm and terminal design and debottlenecking

    • This service focuses on the layout, capacity optimization, and operational improvements of tank farms and terminals to enhance storage and distribution of liquids like oil, chemicals, or gases.

  • Oil and gas gathering, transmission and distribution pipelines

    • These services involve designing and managing pipelines that collect, transport, and distribute oil and gas, ensuring efficiency, safety, and compliance with industry standards.

  • Pipeline pigging facilities

    • Pipeline pigging facilities are designed to maintain and inspect pipelines, using pigs to clean, remove debris, and check for integrity issues, ensuring smooth and reliable pipeline operations.

  • Chemical Storage and handling facilities

    • This involves designing and building facilities for safe and efficient storage, transfer, and handling of chemicals, ensuring environmental safety and compliance with regulations.

  • Support for regulatory applications- new facility and existing facility upgrades

    • This service provides technical and engineering support for regulatory applications required for new facilities and upgrades to existing ones, ensuring compliance with environmental, safety, and operational standards.

  • Professional Engineering expert witness services

    • These services provide expert engineering opinions and testimony for legal cases, disputes, or investigations, leveraging professional experience and technical expertise.

  • Technical studies, planning, engineering, design and construction support

    • This comprehensive service involves feasibility studies, detailed planning, engineering design, and support throughout the construction phase to ensure project success.

  • Owners engineering support

    • Owner's engineering support involves acting on behalf of the owner to oversee project design, construction, and execution, ensuring alignment with project goals, budgets, and schedules while maintaining quality standards.
